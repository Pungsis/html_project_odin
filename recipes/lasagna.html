Broccoli cheese casserole

Descripcion

My mom made this broccoli

cheese casserole every Thanksgiving when I was little. We kids could never get enough! If you have children or have some coming to visit you as guests this Thanksgiving, I guarantee that they will eat (and enjoy) this veggie dish. It's also fabulous with a Christmas ham.

Ingredientes

  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Â¼ cup chopped onion
  • 3 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped broccoli
  • salt and pepper to tast

Preparacion

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Butter a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Whisk condensed soup, mayonnaise, egg, and onions together in a medium mixing bowl until combined
  3. Place frozen broccoli into a very large mixing bowl and break it up if necessary; add soup mixture and mix well to coat. Sprinkle with cheese and mix well; spread mixture
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes to 1 hour.
